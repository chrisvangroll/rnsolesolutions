About Me

I graduated from UW Oshkosh with a bachelors of science in nursing in 2004. I have had a variety of experiences in my 20 year nursing career from working on a medical surgical floor to case management in Family Care. I completed the nurse’s foot and nail course in 2023 at UW Eau Claire’s Continuing Education program and became a Certified Foot Care Nurse (CFCN®) by the Wound, Ostomy and Continence Nursing Certification Board.
